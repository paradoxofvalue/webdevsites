HOW TO DEVELOP A WEBSITE? WEB DEVELOPMENT PROCESS:

  1. Objectives-setting
    Development of a website includes determination of business objectives for successful development and further efficient usage.
  2. Project requirements
    Any technical work presupposes project requirements. Before the beginning of the development, our specialists form project requirements that ensure a positive result (high profit).
  3. Prototyping
    Formation of prototypes is necessary and important for development websites because it allows for creating more user-friendly website interfaces that will attract a lot more visitors and clients.
  4. Design development
    We use different types of markups: fixed, flexible and responsive. We advise our clients to use the last one. The best web development websites are responsive, as it allows us to make a resource convenient to use on tablet, PC and mobile phone.
  5. Markup
    Development of a website includes determination of business objectives for successful development and further efficient usage.
  6. Programming
    At this stage we create all pages and check the functionality of every website element. All the modules, stated in the project requirement, are implemented and the markup is added to the administrative panel. Our web development sites are based on different CMS (Magento, OpenCart, Symfony, etc.) that correspond to your needs and taste, as they are powerful and have a great number of available functions.
  7. Filling with content
    The last stage of website development is filling the website with qualitative and non-duplicated content that will give you a chance for further promotion that will bring much profit and success to any activity you are running.
